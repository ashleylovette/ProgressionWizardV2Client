PROGRESSION WIZARD

Made for songwriters by songwriters

Progression Wizard allows you to identify chords based on the notes you're playing on your instrument using Tonal API. You can also save your chords as songs to your account and even follow other songwriters for inspiration. Sign up now to use these features!
